LOST FORREST

A tranquil escape into nature! The Lost Forest is a lush, shaded trail with hanging vines, tree-top walkways, and hidden animals around every turn.

Things to Look Out For:

  • Sloths hanging out in the treetops
  • Colorful parrots mimicking visitors
  • Interactive treehouse lookout

WHERE CAN YOU FIND IT
